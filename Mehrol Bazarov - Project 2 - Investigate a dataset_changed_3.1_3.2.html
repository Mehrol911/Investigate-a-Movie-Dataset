The Dataset¶

TMDB Movies Dataset is named tmdb-movies.csv, which contains observations of 10k+ movies like title, budget, revenue, cast, director, tagline, keywords, genres, release date, runtime and so on. If you are interested in movies and want to learn interesting things about them, this dataset is a great resource to explore. There are 21 columns in this dataset.

  • id: Unique identifier for each movie
  • imdb_id: Unique identifier of each movie on IMDb
  • popularity: Popularity score of the movie
  • budget: Budget of the movie in dollars
  • revenue: Revenue generated by the movie
  • original_title: Original title of the movie
  • cast: Name of the actors and actresses who acted in the movie
  • homepage: Website of the movie.
  • director: Name of the director who directed the movie
  • tagline: Movie’s tagline if any.
  • keywords: The keywords or tags related to the movie.
  • overview: Brief description of the movie.
  • runtime: Duration of the movie in minutes
  • genres: The genres of the movie.
  • production_companies: Production companies involved in making the movie
  • release_date: Release date of the movie
  • vote_count: Number of votes received by the movie
  • vote_average: Average rating of the movie
  • release_year: Release year of the movie
  • budget_adj: Budget of the movie in terms of 2010 dollars, accounting for inflation
  • revenue_adj: Revenue of the movie in terms of 2010 dollars, accounting for inflation
